Hi, I'm Joell.

I'm a passionate UX Designer and experienced Product Manager and I love creating products that make a positive social impact, especially in Healthcare.

From Virginia to Colorado

Raised outside of Richmond, VA; I followed my love for mountains out west and settled in Denver, CO. I graduated Cum Laude from James Madison University in 2008 and completed a Front End Development course at General Assembly in 2016. In addition to formal education, I'm a student of my craft and incorporate the teachings of Steve Krug, Kent Beck and Jim Collins into my work.

I Bring Teams Together

Using my study of psychology, sociology and human communication, I remove barriers to allow a cross-functional team to collaborate more, communicate often and stay flexible by creating simple, intuitive designs to solve our toughest user challenges.

My Values

I believe that technology should be used to empower others and try to follow that credo myself, in and out of the office. I love being involved in my local community and am especially invested in volunteering to alleviate homelessness, improve addiction treatment and support education.
