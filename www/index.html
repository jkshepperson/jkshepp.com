Hi there, I'm Joell.

I'm a passionate UX Architect, seasoned Product Designer/Manager and I love solving problems.

My Background

With a background in psychology, sociology and conflict resolution, I bring a unique approach to solving problems and have over 10 years experience in healthcare, clinical trials, development services, and entertainment industries and am especially skilled at incorporating the newest AI technologies into our experience. Outside of formal training, I am a student of my craft and prefer well researched books over blog posts.

My Process

There is a method to my madness and that method is rooted in lean, agile product development. I believe that behind the best teams lives a process that encourages and supports positive brainstorming and open, honest communication. My personal style starts with empathy and ends with delivery, with a healthy dose of cross-team collaboration sprinkled throughout.

My Values

In the office and out, my mantra is simple: do the right thing. This means I approach work and projects with honesty, transparency and seek to involve my fellow colleagues early and often in my designs. I am heavily involved in my local community and am especially passionate about improving access to addiction treatment and creating tech opportunities for some of our smaller cities and towns.

My Side Hustles

Although my heart will always have a place for UX, I am also an avid crafts-woman and founded Dune Dog Collective in 2019 to take my passion project more mainstream. At Dune Dog, we focus on recycling glassware and homegoods to make them new again using our sandblaster to create custom designs.
