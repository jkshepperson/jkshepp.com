Nice to meet you, I'm Joell.

I'm a passionate UX Designer, experienced Product Manager and I love creating products that make a positive social impact.

From Virginia to Colorado

Raised outside of Richmond, VA; I followed my love for the mountains and landed in Denver, CO. I graduated Cum Laude from James Madison University in 2008 and completed a Front End Development course at General Assembly in 2016. Outside of formal education, I'm a student of my craft and incorporate the teachings of Steve Krug, Kent Beck and Don Norman into my work.

Bringing Teams Together

Using my study of psychology, sociology and human communication, I remove barriers to allow a cross-functional team to collaborate more, communicate often and stay flexible by creating simple, intuitive designs to solve our toughest user challenges.

Work Hard + Serve Others

In the office and out, I believe in working hard and empowering others. I love to stay involved in my local community and am especially invested in volunteering to alleviate homelessness, improve addiction treatment and support education.
