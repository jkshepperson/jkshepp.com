Hi, I'm Joell.

I love working on products that make a positive impact, especially in healthcare. My specialties include to new levels of effeciency, incorporating UX principles into the design of new products and being the most strategically organized person you've ever met.

Born in CA | Raised in VA

I graduated Cum Laude from James Madison University in 2008 and completed a Front End Development course at General Assembly in 2016. In addition to formal education, I'm a student of my craft and incorporate the teachings of Steve Krug, Kent Beck and Jim Collins into my work.

I bring teams together

Using my study of psychology, sociology and human communication, I remove barriers to allow a cross-functional team to collaborate more, communicate often and stay flexible by creating clear KPIs and a sound Agile development process.

My values are the same in office and out

I believe in the power of technology to help and empower others and follow a personal motto of do the right thing. I love being involved in the local community and am especially invested in volunteering to alleviate homelessness, improve addiction treatment and support education.
