Hi, I'm Joell.

I love working on products that make a positive impact, especially in healthcare. My specialties include leading cross-functional teams to new levels of effeciency, incorporating UX principles into the design of cutting-edge products and being the most organized person you've ever met.

Born Californian, Raised Virginian

I graduated Cum Laude from James Madison University in 2008 and completed a Front End Development course at General Assembly in 2016. In addition to formal education, I'm a student of my craft and incorporate the teachings of Steve Krug, Kent Beck and Jim Collins into my work.

I Bring Teams Together

Using my study of psychology, sociology and human communication, I remove barriers to allow a cross-functional team to collaborate more, communicate often and stay flexible by creating clear KPIs and a sound Agile development process.

My Values

I believe in the power of technology to help empower others and try to follow that credo myself, in and out of the office. I love being involved in my local community and am especially invested in volunteering to alleviate homelessness, improve addiction treatment and support education.
